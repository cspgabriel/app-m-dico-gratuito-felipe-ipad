# Pricing - Clinicafy

Clinicafy has plans for clinics and medical offices that need software médico, prontuário eletrônico, agenda médica and clinic management.

## Básico

- Price: R$ 0/month
- Best for: doctors and small consultórios starting with digital management
- Limits: up to 50 patients
- Includes: patient management, agenda médica, prontuário eletrônico, guias TISS/TUSS and basic clinic workflow

## Profissional

- Price: R$ 149/month
- Best for: growing clinics that need unlimited patients and advanced workflow
- Includes: unlimited patients, reports, PDF exports, recibos médicos, guias TISS/TUSS, team workflow and professional clinic management

## Vitalício

- Price: R$ 2,497 one-time payment
- Best for: clinics that prefer a one-time license instead of monthly billing
- Includes: professional features, clinic management resources and planned WhatsApp/email marketing integrations
